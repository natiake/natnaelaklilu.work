About My Drive

I’m Natnael Aklilu, a Computer Science student at Wolaita Sodo University with big dreams and practical goals. I’m focused on building a future in tech where I can create real solutions, secure contracts, and grow into an entrepreneur who makes an impact across Ethiopia and beyond.

I enjoy exploring how technology actually works, not just using it. I like solving problems, learning new things, and thinking long-term about success. My background keeps me humble, my curiosity keeps me moving, and my faith keeps me grounded.

When I’m not working on code or ideas, I enjoy quiet walks, street food like ertev, and spending time reflecting on what’s next. My goal is simple — to build a life that’s meaningful, independent, and driven by purpose.

Strong goals need solid ground.
